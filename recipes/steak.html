Steak

Recipe credit https://www.foodandwine.com/recipes/butter-basted-rib-eye-steaks

Description

Steak is one of my all time favorite foods.

With this recipe you'll get a great sear and a delciious tasting steak!

Ingredients

  • 2 bone-in ribeye steaks
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 rosemary spring
  • 4 thyme sprigs

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients together, and pat the steaks dry (be sure to let them come to room temp first!)
  2. Season the ribeyes all over with kosher salt and fresh black pepper, patting the seasoning in
  3. Take a large cast-iron skillet and place it on a medium-high heat with the canola oil
  4. Add steaks and cook for roughly 5 minutes
  5. Lower the heat to medium and add butter, thyme, garlic, and rosemary to the skillet
  6. Baste the steak with the browned, herb-infused butter for another 5-7 minutes depending on desired doneness
  7. Take the steak out and place it on a cutting board allowing it to rest before cutting and serving, enjoy!
