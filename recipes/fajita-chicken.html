Fajita Chicken Recipe

Description

This is a very quick and flavorful dish to make!

Perfect to be served with tacos or rice, and great for meal prep.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 3 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lime

Fajita Seasoning

  • 1/2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

For Serving

  • tortillas
  • pico de gallo

Steps

  1. Add fajita seasoning ingredients to a bowl and combine
  2. Cover both sides of chicken breast with seasoning
  3. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat and place chicken breasts, cooking for 7-8 minutes each side
  4. Prepare vegetables (bell peppers & onion)
  5. Remove chicken and placed vegetables into pan with generous olive oil
  6. Slice chicken breast and combine, enjoy!
