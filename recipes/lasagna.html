Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe is simple to make but has great flavor, and uses ingredients you probably already have!

It's also perfect for a quick last-minute dinner or meal prep.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15oz ricotta chese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 lb italian sausage
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomatoe paste

Steps

Prep Work

  1. Combine all of the cheese filling ingredients

Make the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil on medium heat and add diced onions.
  2. Add ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high
  3. Add chicken broth and clean up browned bits at the bottom
  4. Add marinara sauce and tomato paste, bring to a boil and then simmer.

Preheat Oven and Cook Lasagna Noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Begin boiling large pot of salted pasta water for noodles
  3. Lay cooked noodles flat and begin assembling!
